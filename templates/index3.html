Welcome to the Search in Video Engine — Face Detection tool !

This powerful tool helps you detect a specific person in one or more videos by comparing faces against a reference image.

It is useful in finding your own appearances in long event videos — for example, locating your moments in a video without watching the entire thing! This system delivers accurate, fast, and flexible results.

Simply upload your photo and the videos, and the tool will highlight every scene you're in — with control over the detection sensitivity using a threshold.
